About Me

I am currently studying on a part-time web development bootcamp with The Developer Academy.

Most of my career to date has been in the world of education and community, with a particular focus on employer engagement. I have experience across the private, voluntary and public sectors, with my current role being in Local Authority.

I decided to retrain as working with local employers in the digital sector showed me that not only do I have many of the skills they are looking for, but also that I love the values and ways of working in the sector! I am enjoying the course so far, and look forward to the opportunities available to me when I graduate.

Originally from London, I now live in Sheffield with my wife Heather and son Leo, and when not working or training I can usually be found at gigs, making music or playing video games.
